Four pillars hold up
every verification.

Voucha is built on the Squad Hackathon's pillars — AI automation, financial innovation, data, and impact — and every screen makes one of them visible to the user.

I.

AI Automation

OCR + seal vision + signature pattern matching reads each certificate in 2.4 seconds. Reasoning strings ship with every result — no opaque black box.

II.

Financial Innovation

Squad APIs power per-verification micropayments. A ₦1,250 fee replaces ₦12k+ legal/registrar costs, with transparent splits paid to the source institution.

III.

Use of Data

Every verification trains the next. Market-context pattern recognition compares this certificate against thousands from the same year and institution.

IV.

Impact Potential

Credential fraud costs Nigeria an estimated ₦400 B / year. Every Voucha verification routes a transparent share back to the source institution.
